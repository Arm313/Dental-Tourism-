How We Work?

Step 1 : Online application

Here you fill in all the required information about oral cavity. If you have your oral X-ray, OPG, Dental CT image, you can send it to this e-mail: info@dentalTourismArmenia.am. The preliminary plan of treatment will be made and discussed with you.

Step 2 : Final plan of treatment

Final plan of treatment will be made. We welcome any questions you may have. The answers or the feedback will be sent you within 48 hours.

Step 3 : Hotel booking and arrival

After decision has been made, we will finalize your visit days and offer you our partner hotels. The booking and hotel transportation is also our responsibility. We will also provide you with the transport from the hotel to our clinic. You will get a mobile phone to communicate with us.

Step 4 : Consultation

Consultation before treatment. You will meet your dentist to discuss the treatment schedule.

Step 5 : Final plan of treatment

Final plan of treatment will be made. We welcome any questions you may have. The answers or the feedback will be sent you within 48 hours.

Step 6 : Departure

The last day of your visit to the clinic we will carry out the final inspections. You will be accompanied to the airport by our staff.
